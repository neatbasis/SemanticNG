**Instructions**

For each posting, record the raw value in the centre column and circle
the gap outcome. The right column shows the expected ontology home.
Conflicts between expected home and gap outcome are the primary signal
for PostingEpistemics.ttl and the gap ledger.

Complete one copy of this worksheet per posting. Annotate at least 5 and
ideally 10 postings before drafting PostingEpistemics.ttl.
